Description

While the medical and bioscience career field continue to rapidly grow in our country, students find limited opportunities to experience and study these fields in high school. Our mission at CSMB is to provide a level playing field and an equality of opportunity to all students – regardless of ethnic, racial or socioeconomic background – to ensure participation in the bioscience industry while engaged in a rigorous college prep environment.
