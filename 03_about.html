High School in Hand

To help students find the school that best fits them

Mission

Our mission is to engage prospective high school students to find their best fit within St. Louis Public Schools by providing an unbiased, student-centered resource that removes barriers in the school-choice process.

Vision

Our vision is to create a bright future for St. Louis by helping all prospective high school students have a well rounded education through choosing schools that match their interests.
