Description

At Clyde C. Miller Career Academy the academic program is rigorous and includes four years of English, three years of Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies. Students must earn 24 units of high school credits in order to graduate from Career Academy. In addition to core level classes, our students must complete an internship in a location that is relative to their technical pathway.
