Description

Opened in 1956 as O'Fallon Technical High School, Gateway was reestablished in 1992 as Gateway Institute of Technology. In 2012, the school was renamed Gateway STEM High School under the leadership of Dr. Beth Bender. With a mission to embrace diversity and inspire ciritcal thinking through innovatinve career and college pathways, Gateway STEM is the largest high school and magnet school in the St. Louis Public School District.
